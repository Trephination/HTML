Dental Caries affects 93%
of the population worldwide

Plaque formation and
tooth acidification is
primarily caused by
Streptococcus mutans

S. mutans communicate
and form biofilms on
the teeth via
Quorum Sensing

Our Project:

Engineer oral cells to sense the signaling peptide
used by S. mutans in quorum sensing

Secrete anti-biofilm proteins that stop tooth decay
at the source
